Classic travel connection is a travel agency commited simply— to you


Here's how the magic happens.

01— travel Consultation

Priority number one: We take the time to really get to know you. We ask the right questions to understand your priorities, desired atmosphere, and the kinds of activities you enjoy. Because we are already familiar with destinations all over the world, we then play “experience matchmaker” aligning your desires with just the right locale.


This initial consultation can be done in person, by phone, video conference, or even email. We work with you in the way that best suits you and your lifestyle. After getting to know you, we begin crafting your dream travel experience.


02— PERSONALIZED PLANNING & CONCIERGE SUPPORT

As we begin working together, you will have timely responses to all of your questions and concerns. We are available to you via phone, email, or in person, throughout the personalized planning process. We are committed to finding that perfect destination for you, based on your preferences, needs, and your desired spending plan.


We lean on our deep knowledge of destinations and a Rolodex of local contacts to customize travel experiences based on your special interests and needs.


You will receive comprehensive, easy to navigate travel proposals, highlighting the details of your upcoming vacation.


03— IMMERSIVE ITINERARY REVIEW

Once your vacation is confirmed, you will receive a detailed itinerary outlining your travel plans and financial specifics. You will receive reminders via email regarding upcoming payments that are due. You will also receive informational emails specific to your destination that will help prepare you to travel. You’ll receive packing lists, dress codes, cultural information, and a detailed checklist on how to be 100% ready for your vacation.


We obsess over every last detail on your behalf.


04— WHEELS UP (WITH US ON STANDBY EVERY STEP OF THE WAY)

And last but certainly not least, when it’s finally time for you to travel, we are available to you 24/7 for any emergency or unexpected travel needs. As you know, life happens and travel can be unpredictable at times, but we are always here for you.
